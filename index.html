ABOUT US

VISION is a Management Development company established in 2018 in Cairo. VISION is specialized in Training & Development Services, Human Resources Outsourcing (Recruitment & Payroll with whole Human Resource Services), International Events and Consulting Services.

Who We Are

VISION is a “Management Development” company that provides integrated solutions to help companies grow and learn more rapidly than ever before in today’s whirlwind of a market place. VISION consists of three divisions: Training & Development, Marketing & Management Consultancy and Recruitment Services. Each of these divisions operates as a separate company with its own staff and resources
